Introduction

August 20, 2005

Well I made it 1200 miles already, and I passed through some interesting places on the way: Walla Walla, WA, Magic City, ID, Bountiful, UT, Last Chance, CO, Why, AZ and Truth or Consequences, NM.

July 14, 2005

I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today: “Passing cars, When you can’t see, May get you, A glimpse, Of eternity.” I defnitely won’t be passing any cars.

June 2, 2005

My frst day of the trip! I can’t believe I fnally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I’m on a Segway, I wasn’t able to bring a whole lot with me: cellphone, iPod, digital camera, and a protein bar. Just the essentials.As Lao Tzu would have said, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.
